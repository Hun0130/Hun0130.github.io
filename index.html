Research Domain

Cyber-Physical Artificial Intelligence (CPAI)

A research direction unifying AI (the Brain), ROS 2 (the Nerve), and CPS (the Body) to realize Embodied Intelligence systems that remain reliable under real-world uncertainty and resource constraints.
